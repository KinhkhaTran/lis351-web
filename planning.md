# A3 Planning Document - Kinhkha Tran

## Part I, Step 1: Planning

### 1. Purpose

This is a personal portfolio website showcasing my skills, projects, and background as a creative problem solver in web design and development. The page serves as a professional landing page to introduce myself to potential employers, collaborators, and clients, highlighting my abilities in design, development, and strategic thinking.

### 2. Audience

The primary audience for this website includesx:
- Recruiters and hiring managers looking to evaluate my skills and experience
- Potential collaborators interested in working on projects together
- Clients seeking web design and development services
- Peers and classmates who want to learn more about my work

This audience was chosen because a portfolio website is primarily a professional tool for career advancement and networking opportunities.
